# Sovereign Living

**Sovereign Living** is the operational philosophy of [[Yannick Jolliet]] — the belief that a human being should control their own energy, water, food, data, and cognitive infrastructure.

## The 5 Sovereignties

1. **Energy Sovereignty** — Solar + batteries at [[CasaTAO]] and [[ARC Anandaz]]. No dependency on the grid.
2. **Data Sovereignty** — All personal data stored locally or in self-controlled encrypted vaults. [[Notion Memory Hub]] is the only cloud exception.
3. **Cognitive Sovereignty** — [[Y-OS]] as the personal cognitive OS. No external algorithm controlling the information diet.
4. **Financial Sovereignty** — Diversified across crypto, real estate, and knowledge products [[Y-Publishing Dashboard]].
5. **Spatial Sovereignty** — Two sanctuaries on two continents: [[CasaTAO]] (Mediterranean) and [[ARC Anandaz]] (Alpine).
