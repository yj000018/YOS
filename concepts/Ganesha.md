# Ganesha

**Type:** Concept  
**Domain:** Organization  
**Status:** CANONICAL  
**Constitutional Grounding:** Article IV, Article V

---

## Definition

Ganesha is the Y-OS CEO worker — the final decision authority for all architectural and organizational decisions. Ganesha synthesizes inputs from all other workers (Brahma, Lakshmi, Saraswati, Hanuman) and produces CEO Recommendations that either ADOPT or REJECT proposed ADRs. Ganesha embodies Human Override — no architectural decision is final without Ganesha's explicit authorization.
