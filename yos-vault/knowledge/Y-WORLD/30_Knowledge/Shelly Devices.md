# Shelly Devices
Wi-Fi smart home relays and TRVs. Used across [[CasaTAO]] and [[ARC Anandaz]].
TRVs control [[ARC Heating System]]. Relays manage lighting and appliances.
All integrated via [[Home Assistant CasaTAO]] and [[Home Assistant ARC]].
