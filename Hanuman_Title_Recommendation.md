# Title Recommendation: Hanuman

**Status:** Official | **Date:** 2026-06-12 | **Owner:** CODO (Saraswati)

## Context
The current formal title is **Lead Developer (Hanuman)**. A review was requested to evaluate if "Lead Builder" or "Chief Builder" would better serve the universal mission of Y-OS.

## Analysis

| Title Option | Connotation | Y-OS Alignment |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lead Developer** | Strongly associated with software engineering and coding. | **Moderate.** Accurate for current capabilities (Python, scripts), but artificially limits the perception of the role as Y-OS expands into content, organization, and physical systems. |
| **Lead Builder** | Associated with construction, assembly, and creation of any type of artifact. | **High.** Perfectly captures the universal nature of the role. A builder can build code, documents, data pipelines, or organizational structures. |
| **Chief Builder** | Implies a C-level executive position. | **Low.** Hanuman is an execution engine, not an executive. The "Chief" title should be reserved for the Strategic and Orchestration layers (CEO, CSO, COO, CODO, Chief Architect). |

## Recommendation

**Future Evolution to "Lead Builder (Hanuman)"**

The title should remain "Lead Developer" for v1, as it accurately reflects the immediate operational reality (writing Python, interacting with APIs). 

However, as Brahma's capabilities expand into Universal Design (ADR-0010), Hanuman's title should evolve to **Lead Builder** in v2.0 to reflect their status as the universal execution engine of Y-OS, capable of constructing any designed artifact, not just software. The "Chief" prefix must be avoided to maintain hierarchical clarity beneath the COO.
