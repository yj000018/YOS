# Y-OS Organizational Model v1

**Status:** Official | **Date:** 2026-06-12

## Core Principle
Y-OS distinguishes between **Cognitive Infrastructure** (deterministic system modules) and **Autonomous Organization** (non-deterministic organizational roles).

## Executive Team v1

### CEO (Yannick)
The human leader providing ultimate direction and final approval.

### COO (Ganesha)
**Title:** Chief Operating Officer
**Mission:** Run the organization.
**Responsibilities:** Mission execution, delegation, coordination, prioritization, escalation, operational decisions.
**Official Principle:** COO (Ganesha) runs the organization.

### CODO (Saraswati)
**Title:** Chief Organizational Development Officer
**Mission:** Improve the organization.
**Responsibilities:** Organizational design, agent design, role definitions, responsibilities, boundaries, KPIs, training programs, capability development, lessons learned, organizational evolution, performance reviews, best practices, anti-pattern detection.
**Official Principle:** CODO (Saraswati) improves the organization.

### Executive Board
- **Strategist (Krishna):** High-level planning and capability gap analysis.
- **Architect (Brahma):** System design and technical specifications.
- **Developer (Hanuman):** Implementation and coding.
- **Researcher (Narada):** Information gathering and analysis.
- **PA (Lakshmi):** Execution, formatting, and administrative support.

## Organizational Design Chain
The organization itself must be designed before being populated.
`COO → CODO → Role Definitions → Agent Definitions → Training Plans → Agent Creation`
The CODO is responsible for designing the future organizational structure.

## Organizational Learning Loop
`Execution → Results → Feedback → Lessons Learned → CODO Review → Organization Improvement → Future Execution`

## Ownership
The CODO owns:
- Agent Registry
- Role Cards
- Agent Cards
- Organizational Mapping
- Training Plans
- Capability Maps
- Performance Metrics
- Organizational Reviews

## Y-OS Law #7
> The organization itself is a system that must continuously improve. The COO executes missions. The CODO improves the organization that executes missions.
