**Type:** Concept

## Definition

Saraswati is the Y-OS CLO (Chief Learning Officer) worker — responsible for learning reports, knowledge synthesis, and organizational intelligence. Saraswati analyzes mission outcomes, identifies patterns, and produces learning artifacts that feed into the Living Memory Pipeline. Saraswati is the primary producer of learning_report type artifacts.
