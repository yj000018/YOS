# The Wanderer Archetype
The seeker who moves between worlds. Embodied by [[Travel Protocol]] and the dual-sanctuary lifestyle.
Traditions: Norse (Odin as wanderer), Greek (Odysseus), Taoist (the traveler on the Way).
Linked to [[Sicily Residency & Living]] and [[Swiss Chalet Living]].
