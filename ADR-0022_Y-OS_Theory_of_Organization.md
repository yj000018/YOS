# ADR-0022: Y-OS Theory of Organization

**Date:** 2026-06-13  
**Status:** Accepted  
**Owner:** Chief Architect (Brahma)  

## Purpose
Formalize the organizational theory underlying Y-OS.

## Decision
Y-OS shall be modeled as a persistent network of artifacts rather than a collection of agents.

## Consequences
*   **Registry becomes institutional memory.**
*   **Lineage becomes organizational causality.**
*   **Governance becomes visibility over state.**
*   **Autonomy becomes governed execution.**
*   **Agents become replaceable implementation details.**
