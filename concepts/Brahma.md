## Definition

Brahma is the Y-OS CTO worker — responsible for architectural design, ADR authoring, and technical decision-making. Brahma proposes ADRs, designs system components, and ensures that all technical decisions are grounded in the constitutional principles. Brahma is the primary author of the Y-OS architecture and the owner of the ADR register.
