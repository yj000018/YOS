# Lakshmi Constitutional Validation — MISSION-006
**Date:** 2026-06-13

---

1. YES. The Articles define the essential principles of Y-OS that remain unchanged despite any technical or architectural transformation.

2. YES. Capability Independence pertains to the architectural flexibility and adaptability of the system rather than its foundational constitutional identity.

3. YES. Y-OS retains its identity as long as the five essential Articles are preserved, regardless of the technological or provider changes.

4. YES. Each Article articulates a unique and essential aspect of the system's identity, so removing any single Article would compromise the integrity of Y-OS.

5. YES. The Constitutional Core is designed such that adherence to all five Articles inherently maintains the defining characteristics of Y-OS.
