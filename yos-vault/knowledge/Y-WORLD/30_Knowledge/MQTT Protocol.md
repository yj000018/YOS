# MQTT Protocol
Lightweight IoT messaging protocol. The nervous system of [[CasaTAO]] and [[ARC Anandaz]] smart home layers.
Broker: Mosquitto on [[CasaTAO MiniPC]]. Consumers: [[Home Assistant CasaTAO]], [[Home Assistant ARC]], [[n8n Cognitive Automation]].
