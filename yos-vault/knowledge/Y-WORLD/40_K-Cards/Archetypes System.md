# Archetypes System

The **Archetypes System** is Yannick's personal symbolic grammar — a cross-cultural map of universal human archetypes drawn from Norse, Egyptian, Japanese, Greek, and Taoist traditions.

## Purpose

- Provides a **symbolic vocabulary** for naming projects, spaces, and cognitive roles.
- Grounds [[Y-WORLD]] in timeless human patterns rather than ephemeral tech trends.
- Informs the naming of sanctuaries: [[CasaTAO]] (Tao = the Way), [[ARC Anandaz]] (Arc = sacred container, Anandaz = bliss).
